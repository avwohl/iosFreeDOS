## Games NOT Included (and why)

The following games were considered but excluded:

### Excluded - Require 386 Protected Mode (incompatible with emulator)

These are confirmed freeware but require a DOS extender (DOS4GW, CWSDPMI, RTM)
which our 8088/80186 real-mode emulator does not support:

- **Tyrian 2000** (1995): Freeware by Jason Emery (2004), GPL source (OpenTyrian).
  Ref: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tyrian_(video_game)
- **One Must Fall: 2097** (1994): Freeware by Diversions Entertainment (1999).
  Ref: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/One_Must_Fall:_2097
- **Overkill** (1992): CC BY-ND 3.0 by author Ste Cork (2008).
  Ref: https://www.classicdosgames.com/game/Overkill.html

### Excluded - Licensing concerns

- **DOOM / Wolfenstein 3D (shareware):** Shareware license explicitly prohibits
  charging. Bundling in an App Store product may violate license terms.
  Rights held by id Software / Microsoft / ZeniMax.
- **Elder Scrolls: Arena / Daggerfall:** Requires 386 protected mode + DOS
  extender (incompatible with emulator). Rights held by Bethesda / Microsoft.
- **Commander Keen:** Shareware only; still actively sold on Steam.
- **Raptor: Call of the Shadows:** Still commercially sold by Mountain King Studios.
- **Crystal Caves / Secret Agent:** Still commercially sold on Steam/GOG.
- **Grand Theft Auto 1:** Free download discontinued. Take-Two is litigious.
- **Any "abandonware":** No legal standing for redistribution.
